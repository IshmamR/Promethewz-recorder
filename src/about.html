Promethewz

screen recorder

About:

This screen recorder is a free and open-source application built on Electron.js framework. It's easy to install and easy to use.

You can find all the codes for this app at https://github.com/IshmamR/Promethewz-screen-recorder

For any issues and bugs, create an issue on the GitHub repo, or e-mail me at ishmam785@gmail.com

How to use:

 Choose screen to record or capture
 Opens camera
 Stop playing video
 Starts recording after choosing a screen or camera
 Stops recording and saves the recorded video
 Capures a screenshot
 Saves capured screenshot
 Closes screenshot
